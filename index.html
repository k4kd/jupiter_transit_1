Who is Brihaspati?

Guru of Devtagan — karak of wisdom, wealth, marriage, and children. The Vedas call him a drop of nectar in times of difficulty.

Brihaspati is the spiritual preceptor of the gods — the planet of expansion, hope, and divine possibility. He governs higher knowledge, ethics, fortune, and the blessings of teachers. Vedic tradition teaches that no Mangal Karya — no auspicious beginning, no major undertaking — can fully succeed without his blessing.

Why Cancer is sacred

Cancer (Kark) is Jupiter's uchcha — its exaltation sign. Here, Jupiter operates at absolute peak strength.

Every planet has one sign where its energy reaches maximum potency. For Jupiter, that sign is Cancer — ruled by the Moon. When Jupiter sits with the Moon's energy, the auspicious Gaj Kesari yoga is born, wisdom meets compassion, and every prayer, mantra, and ritual carries multiplied power.

Jupiter stays in each rashi for only ~13 months. This exalted window will not return until 2038.
