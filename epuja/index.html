WHAT IS GURU GOCHAR?

Once in every 12 years, the planet Guru Brihaspati enters Kark Rashi (Cancer). This is the sign where Guru becomes the strongest. Vedic tradition calls this his "exalted home."

Guru Brihaspati is the divine teacher of the gods. Devotees pray to him for wisdom, family peace, marriage, children, money, and good decisions in life. His soft and kind gaze is believed to ease the heavy worries we carry for years.

When Guru sits in his own special home, prayers and sacred rituals offered during this time are believed to carry deeper grace. This is why temples across India light extra lamps, and devotees come together to seek his blessings for the year ahead.
